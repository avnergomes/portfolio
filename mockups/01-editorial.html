§ I — Practice

The shape of the work.

My practice sits at the seam of three disciplines that rarely share an office — remote sensing, statistical modelling, and the kind of business analytics that gets a dashboard read in a board meeting.

For a fund running EUDR compliance, that means a Sentinel-2 pipeline that produces auditable deforestation evidence per supplier polygon. For a cooperative in Paraná, it means a forecast for grain prices that the commercial team trusts enough to hedge against. For a Qatari client, it means a LiDAR-derived terrain model handed off with the methodology fully traceable.

The throughline is the same: decision-grade deliverables, methodology stated. Nothing said with more confidence than the data supports.

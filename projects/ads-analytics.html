Multi-Channel Ads & Ticket Sales Analytics

Unified advertising analytics across Google, Meta, and TikTok, connecting ad spend to ticket sales performance for an events company.

Marketing Analytics
2024
Latin America

Project Overview

An events company was running advertising campaigns across Google, Meta, and TikTok but lacked visibility into which platform and campaign was actually driving ticket sales. The project unified data from all ad platforms with ticketing system data to provide clear ROI attribution.

The analytics solution enabled the marketing team to identify top-performing campaigns, optimize budget allocation, and understand geographic performance patterns across different cities.

For the first time, the team could see which platform and which city drove the best ROI per ticket sold, enabling data-driven budget optimization.

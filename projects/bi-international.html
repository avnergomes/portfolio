International Sales BI Dashboard

Custom business intelligence dashboard for a European fabric company, providing spatial distribution patterns and sales performance insights.

Business Intelligence
2023
UK / Germany

Project Overview

Built a comprehensive business intelligence dashboard for a European fabric company that needed to understand sales patterns across UK and Germany markets. The solution provides real-time insights by product, customer segment, and geographic region.

The dashboard integrates multiple data sources and includes geographic heat maps, time series analysis, and customer segmentation views that the sales team reviews every Monday morning.

Spatial distribution patterns by product, customer, region, and sales representative became visible for the first time, enabling data-driven decision making.

Deep Grain - Agricultural Assessment Ecuador

Ground-truth agricultural assessment combining field photography with geospatial analysis for investment-grade land evaluation.

Geospatial
2025
Ecuador

Project Overview

Conducted comprehensive agricultural land assessment in Ecuador's North Esmeraldas and Guayas/El Oro regions. The project combined geotagged field photography with satellite imagery analysis to evaluate crop suitability and yield potential for investment decisions.

Field data collection involved systematic documentation of soil conditions, existing crops, infrastructure, and terrain characteristics across multiple properties.

Investment-grade agricultural assessment supporting land acquisition decisions with verified field conditions and spatial context.
